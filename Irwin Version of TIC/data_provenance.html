What is Data Provenance?

Data provenance refers to the document which is the records of the inputs, entities, systems, and processes that influence data of interest, in effect providing a historical record of the data and its origins"[SIIS].

Data Provenance

It is a series of transformations applied to an input data. A data provenance can be an advancement of scientific research. It acts as an insight that can be applied to many other fields such as Business, eScience, and eCommerce.

Data provenance in eScience
Data provenance takes an important role in eScience. eScience is a scientific research having to use computataional tools. Also known as "Science increasingly performed through distributed global collaborations enable by the Internet, using very large data collections, tera-scale computing resources and high performance visualization"[Senior, 2002]. Data provenance aids in understanding the results of eScience research by enabling experiment to be verified and identifies which components to rerun. Moreover, it facilitates sharing of analysis among collaborating scientists.

Why is Data Provenance important in eScience?
1. Data provenance tells you the conditions and transformation that are used.
2. Data provenance helps understanding the experiment design and the rationale behind the experiment.
3. Data provenance helsp in tracking the past experiemnts, their related datasets, and the resulting publications.


How do we capture Data Provenance?
1. Compute provenance is a database approach that uses inverse operations of database program commands.
2. Recording provenance is a commonly used technique in data provenance. It includes scientific workflows and database program's execution and command lines that applies to current and future analysis.
3. Recover Provenance also known as reconstruction connects available information to approximate what transpired "close-enough" that applies to past analysis.
